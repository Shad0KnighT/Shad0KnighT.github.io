What they do

Studio C has assembled a talented ensemble of actors whose comedic prowess and diverse talents have endeared them to audiences worldwide. From the sharp wit of Whitney Call and Mallory Everton to the versatile humor of Jason Gray and Matt Meese, each member brings a unique flavor to the group's sketches and performances. Adam Berg's physical comedy and Stacey Harkey's charismatic presence add further depth to their dynamic repertoire. With Natalie Madsen's infectious energy and Jeremy Warner's knack for memorable characters, Studio C's cast excels in creating entertaining and relatable content that resonates with fans of all ages. Together, they continue to captivate audiences with their comedic genius and collaborative spirit, making Studio C a beloved staple in the world of sketch comedy.
